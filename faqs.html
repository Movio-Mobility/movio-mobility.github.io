Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the PowerPod battery-pack costs? Free!
We do not sell the PowerPod to the consumers, we make sure our users don't get to deal with the financial burdens of battery-ownership, such as its initial and replacement costs.
Instead, we lease it to the user at a pay-as-you-go model.
How does the 'pay-as-you-go' model work? This model allows consumers to pay only for the services or products they use, without any upfront costs or long-term commitments.
Charges are typically based on usage or consumption, making it a flexible and cost-effective option.
This model is ideal for customers who prefer to manage expenses based on actual usage.
Payments are made periodically, depending on the terms of service.
Is the PowerPod compatible with my EV? Our solution is compatible with a wide range of electric two-wheelers, including low-speed, mid-speed, and high-speed models.
Contact us to confirm the compability of your EV with our products.
How much range will I get with a single PowerPod? A single PowerPod is 1.4kWh, which is enough for your daily commute usage depending upon the efficiency of your electric vehicle.
