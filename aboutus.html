The Origins

In the vibrant corridors of Kota, three kindred spirits—Shivanshu, Hriteek, and Vishrut—found each other amid the intense pursuit of JEE coaching. Their shared reverence for technology became the catalyst for a remarkable journey that would shape the future.

Fast forward to college, where Shivanshu and Hriteek embarked on their engineering journeys at VIT Vellore, delving into ECE with a specialization in IoT and Mechanical Engineeirng, respectively. Meanwhile, Vishrut explored the vast landscape of Computer Science in Bangalore.

The trio's college years unfolded like an epic tale, they choreographed an epic narrative, leading the combat robotics team from the obscure fringes to a dazzling 6th position on the national stage. This chapter in their academic journey emerged as a crucible, a forge of challenges that sculpted their mastery in product development, pushing the limits of electronic fortitude in the most formidable environments.

As graduation approached, Shivanshu and Hriteek transitioned from the battle arena to solving real-world problems. Hriteek's path led him to Grab Logistics and Urban Company, where he honed his skills as an Operation Manager, gaining invaluable experience in business operations. Simultaneously, Shivanshu delved into the world of startup innovation, working at NUNAM, a startup specializing in the second life of batteries. Here, he immersed himself in understanding the inner workings of hardware startups and delved deep into the core technologies of batteries. Their journey took a pivotal turn with the addition of Vishrut, who brought a wealth of technical expertise from his roles at Apple and Boeing Research.

At this juncture, Paridhi Sinha, an economics maestro, joined the ensemble. With a background rooted in economics and a keen understanding of business dynamics, she became an integral part of the team.

The four minds converged, birthing GridXenergy, a battery-as-a-service startup, fueled by a singular dream: to make energy storage reliable, affordable, and flexible. Their story is one of synergy, diverse expertise, and a shared vision for a greener, more accessible future. Join the captivating journey at GridXenergy, where they aren't just revolutionizing energy storage; they're empowering the future of mobility.
