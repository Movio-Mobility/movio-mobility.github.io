The Origin

Founded in 2021, Movio Technologies Private Limited (Movio Mobility) was a micro-mobility startup that later on was rebranded as GridXenergy.

At GridXenergy, we are building India's most flexible and innovative battery-as-a-service platform. We offer lightweight battery packs and multiple charging options, including home charging, battery swapping, and access to public fast charging, to address the range anxiety caused by infrastructure limitations that slow EV adoption. Our integrated approach combines battery swapping with traditional charging methods, enhancing reliability, affordability, and flexibility for high-intent users whose livelihoods depend on two-wheelers, as well as two-wheeler enthusiasts.

Our mission is to simplify EV ownership, reduce costs, and alleviate range anxiety, significantly contributing to sustainable mobility and transforming India's EV landscape.
